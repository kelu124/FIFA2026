# Prediction Scoring System

| Outcome | Points |
|---------|--------|
| Exact score (correct scoreline) | 3 pts |
| Correct result (win/draw/loss), wrong score | 1 pt |
| Wrong result | 0 pts |

## Bonus rules

- Correct goalscorer (if tracked): +1 pt per scorer
- Penalty shootout prediction (correct winner): +1 pt

## Total

Points are summed across all matches. Final leaderboard will be posted after the Final (July 19, 2026).
